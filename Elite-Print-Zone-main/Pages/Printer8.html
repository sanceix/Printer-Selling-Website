HP Laser Multifuntion Printer


Easy to use, simple to manage




HP Office Laser Multifunction Printers are designed for SMBs and small workteams in larger companies, delivering enhanced productivity, simplified workflows and reduced costs. This printer is intended to work only with cartridges that have a new or reused HP chip, and it uses dynamic security measures to block cartridges using a non-HP chip.



  • Print, copy, scan, fax
  • Print speed letter: Up to 42 ppm (HP high speed mode) black
  • Auto duplex printing; 50-sheet ADF; 4.3-inch color touchscreen
  • Dynamic security enabled printer
